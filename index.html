About Me

I’m a Computer Science undergraduate at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), affiliated with the University of Westminster. With experience as a UI/UX Design Intern at Sri Lanka Telecom, I’ve led impactful design projects that improved both internal and client-facing solutions.

I specialize in creating intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital experiences using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch. My knowledge of front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) allows me to bridge the gap between design and development.
